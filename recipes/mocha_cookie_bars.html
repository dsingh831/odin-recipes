Description

These chocolaty cookie bars are somewhat of a cross between a brownie and a cookie, with a flavor reminiscent of Mexican hot chocolate. They're best if you err on the side of under- versus over-baking.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon instant espresso
  • 1 tablespoon strong brewed coffee
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened coca powder
  1. Step 1: Preheat oven 350 degrees F
  2. Step 2: Stir espresso powder, coffee, and other wet ingredients
  3. Step 3: Whisk flour, cocoa, cream of tartar, cinnamon
  4. Step 4: Whisk, add to cake tin, and put in oven
